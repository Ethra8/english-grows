About Us

Welcome to English Grows, where we offer high-quality online lessons, tailored by our qualified team of teachers, to meet the diverse needs of both individual learners and companies.

Whether you're an individual looking to advance your skills with flexible lesson packs or a business seeking custom training solutions for your team, we're here to help you succeed.

For B2C customers, we provide a range of options to suit your learning style and schedule. Choose from:

  • Individual Lesson Packs - ideal for those who prefer a one-on-one approach and the flexibility to learn at their own pace.
  • Reduced Group Courses - small group courses with a maximum of six participants, ensuring a collaborative yet focused environment to learn and connect with others.

For B2B clients, we offer customized training programs designed to elevate your team's skills in alignment with your business goals. Simply fill out our contact form on the Companies section, and we'll work with you to create a tailored training plan that enhances your employees' professional growth.

At English Grows, we're committed to delivering effective and engaging online education that empowers individuals and organizations alike.

Join us and take the next step in your learning journey!
